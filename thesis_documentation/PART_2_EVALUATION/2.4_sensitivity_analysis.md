## Key Insight

**All deep learning models (NP, TFT) suffer from overfitting on price prediction.**

The pattern is clear:
- Price is a martingale → complex models overfit noise
- Volatility is predictable → models can exploit clustering

This confirms the theoretical foundation:
- Martingale property: E[price_{t+1} | price_t] = price_t
- Volatility clustering: High vol today → high vol tomorrow (α+β ≈ 0.97)
