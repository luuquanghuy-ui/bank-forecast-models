## Key Insight

**Deep learning models respond differently to price vs volatility:**

- **Price (martingale)**: NP severely overfits (200-600% worse than Naive). TFT is close to Naive but doesn't beat it. Only Hybrid beats Naive.
- **Volatility (clustering)**: TFT exploits patterns via attention (30-34% better than Naive). NP also improves with higher learning rate.

The pattern is clear:
- Price is a martingale → complex models overfit noise (except Hybrid)
- Volatility is predictable → TFT exploits clustering with 30% improvement

This confirms the theoretical foundation:
- Martingale property: E[price_{t+1} | price_t] = price_t
- Volatility clustering: High vol today → high vol tomorrow (α+β ≈ 0.97)
