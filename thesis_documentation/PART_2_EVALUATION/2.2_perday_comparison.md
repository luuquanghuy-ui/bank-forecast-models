## Key Observations

### 1. TFT Wins Volatility (3/3 banks)
- BID: TFT wins with 0.0076 MAE (33.9% better than Naive)
- CTG: TFT wins with 0.0078 MAE (30.6% better than Naive)
- VCB: TFT wins with 0.0062 MAE (31.9% better than Naive)
- Results confirm 4-fold CV: TFT dominates volatility

### 2. Hybrid Wins Price (3/3 banks)
- All 3 banks: Hybrid is best
- XGBoost severely overfits on price (893-1169% worse than Naive)
- NP also overfits (395-1169% worse than Naive)
- Only Hybrid beats Naive on price

### 3. TFT Performs Decently on Price
- Close to Naive performance
- Much better than XGBoost and NP
- But still doesn't beat Hybrid

### 4. Per-Day vs 4-Fold CV
- Per-day: Single 70/30 split
- 4-fold: Expanding window (more robust)
- Both confirm: TFT wins volatility, Hybrid wins price
