# 2.2 Per-Day All Models Comparison

## Overview

Per-day analysis compares ALL models on the same test days, for both price and volatility targets.

**Script:** `run_perday_all_models.py`
**Output:** `perday_all_models/BID_perday_all.csv`, etc.

## Key Observations

### 1. Hybrid Consistently Best for Volatility
- Wins on ALL 3 banks
- 17-30% improvement over naive
- 10-21% improvement over GARCH alone

### 2. TFT Surprisingly Good on Volatility (After Conversion)
- On BID: 0.0093 (only slightly worse than GARCH)
- On VCB: 0.0081 (similar to GARCH)
- But on CTG: 0.0107 (worse than GARCH)

### 3. NP Completely Fails on Volatility
- After converting price to volatility, NP is:
  - BID: 52% worse than Naive
  - CTG: 22% worse than Naive
  - VCB: 62% worse than Naive
- NP cannot predict volatility even when converted

### 4. TFT Vastly Better Than NP on Price
- But still worse than Naive
- CTG is extreme: TFT 5.27 vs Naive 0.34 (15x worse)
